Details

We would like to encourage entries in the following categories: Sangeetham, Natyam, Natikalu, Geyalu, Kavithalu.

  • We will stick to limited max. 12 performances and our sincere intention are to give chance to everyone
  • If there are numerous entries, we encourage group performances verses solo events to accommodate the most
  • The duration for each event can be a maximum of 5 minutes. Skits/ play can last up to max 7 mins.
  • Participants are requested not to participate in more than one event
  • We encourage and strongly recommend the songs that are being performed are in Telugu
  • Performers or group representative is responsible for their costumes, makeup and properties needed

Enrollment

All entries must be submitted by 28th of Feb 2018

Please send in the following details with your entries for cultural events:

  • Name (Solo) / Group name
  • Name(Solo) / Group coordinator Name, contact phone number and email
  • Number of participants and Names: Please include LAST & FIRST NAME
  • Parents Names (Solo Performance): Please add PARENT NAME (Last and First name) and email address
  • Event Description
  • Event Length (duration)
  • Duration/Other Exceptions: If you are planning on a very special event which exceeds the time limit allocated or have any other special needs. Please send us more details with your entry. This is to avoid any last minute changes and reduce any time wastage and redundancies
In previous events natikalu, geyalu and kavithalu entrollments are very less, so we request and encourage taking part in this too.
FITA is looking forward to making the Ugadi 2018 event a grand success with your active participation.
